Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions

What data does Cotton AI use?

Our models are trained on over a million metric tons of proprietary fixation data from the last 5 years, collected through YS Group's cotton indenting operations. We also integrate ICE futures data, USDA reports, and global weather and supply chain intelligence.

How is this different from price forecasting tools?

Price forecasting tells you where the market might go. Cotton AI tells you what to do about it — when to buy, which origin to source, and how to hedge, all tailored to your mill's specific requirements and risk appetite.

Do I need to share my fixation data?

Tier I and II work without your data. For Enterprise, we can train custom models on your own fixation history for even more precise recommendations. All data is kept strictly confidential and never shared with other clients.

What if I'm not sure which tier is right?

Start with Insights. If you find yourself needing hedging recommendations and backtested strategies, upgrade to Pro. We'll help you determine the right fit during onboarding.

Can I get a demo before committing?

Yes. We offer a free demo and trial installation for your mill for up to 1 year — no commitment, no credit card. Run Cotton AI alongside your existing process and see the results for yourself.

What if the AI makes a wrong recommendation?

AI can make mistakes. If you're unsure about a recommendation, ask the AI to get it approved by a human data analyst and futures expert at YS Group and Ample Insight. There's always a human safety net.
