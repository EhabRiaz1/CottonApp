Founder
OY

Omer Yahya

Founder & CEO, Cotton AI Inc.

Omer brings a unique combination of quantitative finance training, hands-on commodity trading experience, and AI product development to Cotton AI. His background spans both sides of the cotton market — the trading floor and the analytics lab.

Through YS Group, Omer has been immersed in the mechanics of international cotton indenting — origin selection, basis negotiation, fixation timing, and the commercial relationships that move raw materials across continents. Cotton AI was born from seeing firsthand how much value is lost when these decisions are made without data.

  • Undergrad Bentley University — B.S. in Corporate Finance & Accounting
  • Industry Executive Director, YS Group
  • Trading Active ICE Cotton No. 2 futures & options positions via StoneX Financial
